Big Time Clock

Privacy Policy — Effective April 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Big Time Clock, an application developed by averyio ("we," "us," or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the Big Time Clock app, how it is used, and how it is protected. By downloading and using Big Time Clock, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. Big Time Clock is designed to function without requiring you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personally identifiable information directly with averyio. All data generated by your use of the app remains entirely on your local device unless otherwise specified below through Apple's standard services.

3. Purchases

Big Time Clock is a paid app with a one-time upfront purchase. It contains no In-App Purchases and no subscriptions.

Payment Processing: Your initial purchase is processed directly and securely by Apple via the App Store.

Data Access: We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID credentials. We only receive anonymized confirmation from Apple that a purchase has taken place.

For more information on how Apple handles your payment data, please review the Apple Privacy Policy.

4. Analytics and Usage Data

We do not use any third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.) within Big Time Clock.

We only have access to anonymized, aggregated analytics provided by Apple App Store Connect. This data is entirely controlled by your iOS device settings and is only shared with us if you have opted in to share app analytics with developers.

This data may include:

  • App crash reports
  • Number of downloads and deletions
  • General usage metrics (e.g., sessions per active device)

This information is strictly used to identify bugs, improve the app's performance, and understand general user engagement. It cannot be used to identify you personally. You can change your analytics sharing preferences at any time in your iOS device Settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements).

5. Third-Party Services and Advertising

No Ads: Big Time Clock does not display any advertisements.

No Tracking: We do not use any third-party tracking software, advertising networks, or SDKs that collect data on our behalf.

No Network Access: Big Time Clock is fully offline. It does not transmit any data from your device to us or to any third party.

6. Permissions

Big Time Clock requests no special permissions. It does not access your location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, notifications, or any other sensitive data on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Big Time Clock does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and publishing the updated policy within the app or on its App Store listing.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please visit our Contact Us page.
